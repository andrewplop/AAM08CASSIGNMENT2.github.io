About Us

BOXING FOR ALL

Discover Your Potential Today

Ares Fight Club has been changing lives all over Singapore since we were established in 2012. Our coaches are certified boxing trainers, with many years of boxing and coaching experience.

​

Ares Fight Club is a BOXING-ONLY club. Here at Ares, we specialise in the sweet science of Western Boxing. We love boxing. We breathe boxing. We live for boxing. We are ALL boxing, good old traditional boxing and we are good at it.

﻿

We cater to both experienced boxers and individuals with no fitness experience. We believe that boxing should be for all - regardless of age, fitness level and experience.

​ ﻿We are the ultimate boxing and fitness challenge. So join us now!.

Directions

By Car If you are coming from Upper Bukit Timah Road, turn into Chun Tin Road and then turn left into Jalan Seh Chuan, followed by a right into Lorong Kilat.

If you are coming from Jalan Jurong Kechil, turn left into Lorong Kilat.

There is street parking along Lorong Kilat, parking available in 2 carparks along Jalan Seh Chuan and a carpark off Toh Yi Drive, near Blk 18.

By Public Transport

The buses that stop along Jalan Jurong Kechil: 61, 66, 77, 157, 173, 174, 970, 985

The buses that stop along Upper Bukit Timah Road: 67, 75, 170, 171, 173, 184, 852, 961

Nearest MRT: Beauty World
